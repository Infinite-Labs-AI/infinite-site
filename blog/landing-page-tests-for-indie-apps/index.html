Landing-page tests indie apps should run first.

Most founders change too much at once. The better approach is to test one high-leverage assumption at a time and keep the page simple enough to learn from.

Test the promise

Does the hero explain the outcome buyers want, or does it describe the product architecture? Start with one promise that a stranger understands in five seconds.

Test the proof

Add one proof block before the first major CTA. That proof can be a metric, workflow screenshot, testimonial, or concrete before/after.

Test the CTA intent

A demo CTA, free trial CTA, and download CTA attract different people. Test the action that matches where your buyer is in the decision.
