SEO + GEO

SEO and GEO for AI CMO products.

Buyers no longer research in one place. They ask Google, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, communities, and peers. A strong AI CMO site needs pages that answer those questions clearly enough to be cited and chosen.

Build around buyer questions

Useful pages answer direct questions: what the product does, who it is for, what it replaces, what it integrates with, and how it compares to alternatives.

Add comparison pages early

Comparison pages are not just competitor pages. They help buyers name the category, understand tradeoffs, and see where your workflow is strongest.

Keep pages connected to product work

The best SEO and GEO briefs should create a page, a landing test, and a content angle. That is how search work becomes a growth loop instead of a content backlog.
