Founder growth

How technical founders get their first customers after launch.

Shipping the product is no longer the rare part. The hard part is turning a working app into a market loop: people discover it, understand it, try it, and tell you why they care.

Start with the places where pain is already public

Do not begin by inventing campaigns. Begin by finding existing conversations: Reddit threads, X posts, comments under competitor launches, search queries, and comparison pages. The best early leads are already describing urgency, budget, or failed alternatives.

Turn each signal into one next move

A good signal should create something useful: a reply angle, landing-page test, SEO page, content post, or product note. If a signal does not produce a next move, it is probably noise.

Make the loop reviewable

Founders still need taste. The scalable pattern is not blind automation; it is prepared work with source context attached. That is the loop Infinite is built around.
