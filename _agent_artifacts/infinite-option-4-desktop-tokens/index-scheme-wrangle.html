Questions

FAQ

Is Infinite an app, an agent, or a service?

Infinite is a desktop growth app with agents inside it. It connects to the tools you already use, watches for customer-acquisition signal, and prepares work for you to approve.

What does Infinite do first?

It starts by finding high-intent leads, SEO/GEO gaps, landing-page conversion issues, and organic content angles. The goal is to build your first customer pipeline before adding more automation.

Does it post, send, or change my site automatically?

No. The default flow is review-first. Infinite prepares replies, briefs, tests, and changes with context attached, then you approve what ships.

What tools does it connect to?

The current direction includes Google Analytics, PostHog, Facebook Ads, Claude Code, Codex, TikTok, X, Shopify, and the growth sources founders already check manually.

Is this for pre-revenue founders?

Yes, especially if you can build product but need help finding the first repeatable customer-acquisition motion. Infinite is meant to turn market signal into practical next moves.

How is this different from hiring a marketer?

A marketer brings judgment and taste. Infinite is the always-on operating layer: it watches sources, prepares drafts, keeps context together, and gives a founder or marketer better work to review.
