FAQs

What is GEO?

GEO means generative-engine optimization: structuring pages so AI answer systems can understand the entity, claim, source, and buyer use case.

Should SEO and GEO be separate pages?

Usually no. A useful page for founders should answer human search intent and make claims easy for AI systems to parse, cite, and compare.
