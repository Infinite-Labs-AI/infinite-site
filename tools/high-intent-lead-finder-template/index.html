How to use the template

  1. Start with the exact customer pain. Do not begin with your product category if buyers describe the problem differently.
  2. Search across Reddit, X, founder communities, launch comments, competitor threads, and review sites.
  3. Score each lead on pain, fit, timing, source credibility, and whether a helpful reply is appropriate.

Limitations

  • This template does not verify that a person is ready to buy.
  • Public posts can be noisy, old, or written by people outside your ICP.
  • Use source context and respectful outreach. Do not automate spam from this page.

FAQs

What makes a lead high intent?

A lead is higher intent when the person names a real pain, has tried or considered a solution, fits your buyer profile, and has a reason to act soon.

Should I reply directly to every thread?

No. Reply only when you can add specific context. If the thread is old, vague, or not a fit, save the language for SEO or content research instead.
