How to use the ideas

  1. Pick one primary hypothesis and write down what would prove it wrong.
  2. Keep the test small enough that a result tells you what changed.
  3. Measure the action that matters: demo request, download, reply, signup, or qualified click.

Limitations

  • The generator does not know your live analytics, sample size, or traffic quality.
  • A test idea is not a statistically valid result.
  • Do not claim lift until the test has enough clean data and a pre-set decision rule.

FAQs

What should founders test first?

Start with the hero message and CTA when visitors understand the category but do not act. Test proof placement when buyers need more confidence.

How many variants should I run?

For most small founder sites, one control and one variant is easier to learn from than a broad multivariate test with thin traffic.
